## The Broader Role

friction-pipeline is the outbound half of a continuous revenue loop. It sends. organic-pipeline receives. The Forensic Dictionary is the shared language between both channels. Neptune's Bite language governs what the inbound sites say — not the other way around.

New workspaces are earned by new TAM entry points (retargeting, lead magnets) — not new diagnostic frameworks. The Forensic Dictionary is universal.
